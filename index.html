Overview of Current Project

My Current project is called On Thin Ice. On Thin Ice is a parkour rage game, inspired by Only Up and Getting Over It
but my game has a unique twist, you can never stop moving and you will change directions when hitting a wall other than
that you cannot turn. You can control the speed that you go with, "a" for slow speed, "s" for regular speed, and "d" for fast movement.
This game requires lots of strategy, regarding when you slowdown and speed up, causing there to be some brain activity while playing.
The main objective of this game is to reach the top in the shortest amount of time possible with a timer in the top right.
